# Madyan Alsenwi

**Research Scientist · SnT, University of Luxembourg**

Research Scientist at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT),
University of Luxembourg, in the SIGCOM group. I work at the intersection of optimization and
machine learning, applied to wireless and satellite communication systems.

My work focuses on decision-making under uncertainty. I use deep reinforcement learning for sequential decisions, Gaussian processes for prediction with calibrated uncertainty, and convex and risk-aware optimization to tie them to the problem at hand: spectrum and power allocation, multi-connectivity across orbits, and energy savings in the radio access network.


### Research interests

- Optimization under uncertainty: convex and non-convex methods, risk-aware and stochastic formulations
- Machine learning: deep reinforcement learning, federated learning, Gaussian processes
- Satellite and non-terrestrial networks; 5G/6G, RIS, Open RAN, UAV
- Digital twins and data-driven decision-making on Earth-observation data

### Working with

Python (PyTorch, NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn), convex optimization, and MATLAB for physical-layer
simulation.
